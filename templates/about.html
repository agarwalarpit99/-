Smart Classes

To cope-up with the modern world, Smart Classes have been going on since last 5 years from V to XII. Traditional as well as new methods of teaching create zeal among the students who like the system very much. The school has the working force of over 120 people who are continuously involved in the well being of the children and the upkeep of the school. Late Shri Haresh Singh Tomar was a noted educationist, thinker and a visionary. He dreamt of an educational system based on values like honest conviction and self confidence. Today, Holy Public School has distinguished itself by realizing the dreams of its founding father of fulfilling the educational needs which
